# Competitor Analysis: Munich HVAC/SHK Market

## Top Direct Competitors

### 1. Mönner GmbH (moenner.de)
- **Profile:** 3rd generation, 70+ years, München-Sendling/Neuhausen
- **Services:** Heizung + Sanitär + Badsanierung (no Elektro)
- **Strengths:**
  - Clean, well-structured site with dedicated landing pages per service
  - SEO-optimized URLs (`/badsanierung-muenchen/`, `/waermepumpe/`)
  - Team pages with personal photos building trust
  - Excellent reviews: 4.28/5 on 11880, "EXCELLENT" on MeinungsMeister
- **Weakness:** No Elektrotechnik — strictly Heizung/Sanitär

### 2. Karl Greiner GmbH (karl-greiner-gmbh.de)
- **Profile:** Since 1961, 25 employees — **closest direct competitor** (all 3 trades)
- **Services:** Elektro + Heizung + Sanitär + Wärmepumpen + PV + Wasseraufbereitung
- **Strengths:**
  - Same 3-trade model as Pertler
  - Forward-looking with renewables (PV, heat pumps)
  - Clean URL structure with service-specific pages
  - Active job postings
- **Differentiator:** Solar/PV offering gives them a modern edge

### 3. Der Rieger GmbH — "Die Bäderschmiede" (derrieger.de)
- **Profile:** 40+ years, Munich's leading bathroom specialist
- **Services:** Premium bathrooms only — but sets the gold standard
- **Strengths:**
  - 3D visualization/planning service
  - Dedicated FAQ section reducing purchase anxiety
  - Referenzen (portfolio) with completed projects
  - Content marketing (topical articles)
  - 2 physical showrooms
  - Award: "Beste Badstudios" since 2016
- **Takeaway:** This is what Pertler's Bäder section should aspire to

### 4. Klaus Kogelmiller (kogelmiller.de)
- **Profile:** Owner-operated Meisterbetrieb, 25+ years, Obersendling
- **Services:** Heizung + Sanitär + Elektro — most structurally similar to Pertler
- **Strengths:**
  - Meisterbetrieb credentials prominently featured
  - Team page with real people
  - Listed on many trade directories
- **Weakness:** Smaller, less polished site — beatable

### 5. J. Baumgartner (j-baumgartner.de)
- **Profile:** 75+ years, broadest service range
- **Services:** Elektro + Gas + Sanitär + Heizung + Bad + Komplettsanierung
- **Strengths:**
  - Widest portfolio including complete renovation
  - Heritage/longevity as trust signal
- **Differentiator:** "Komplettsanierung" as a service

## Emergency Service Competitors (Notdienst)
- **Heizungsnotdienst Dogan** (heizungsnotdienst-dogan.de) — 24h since 2003
- **ISAR Heiztechnik** (isar-heiztechnik.de) — 1 hour arrival within 50km
- **SH Sanitär Heizung** (sh-sanitaer-heizung.de) — 24/7 page
- **Allwartung** (allwartung.de/notdienst) — after-hours emergency

**Key insight:** If Pertler offers Notdienst, a dedicated `/notdienst/` page is a high-intent SEO opportunity none of the general competitors own well.

## Strategic Positioning for Pertler

### Pertler's Unique Advantage
One of very few Munich firms offering **Heizung + Sanitär + Elektro + Bäder** under one roof. The "alles aus einer Hand" message is underused by all competitors.

### Gaps to Exploit (No Competitor Has These)
1. **Online booking** — literally nobody offers proper online scheduling
2. **Page speed** — all competitors run WordPress/slow sites
3. **Schema markup** — none have proper LocalBusiness/Service schema
4. **Modern tech stack** — all are WordPress, all are slow
5. **Smart booking flow** — with urgency detection for emergencies

### What to Steal (Best of Each Competitor)
- From **Mönner:** SEO URL structure with Munich keywords
- From **Rieger:** Project gallery, FAQ section, content marketing approach
- From **Greiner:** Multi-trade positioning, renewable energy messaging
- From **Notdienst specialists:** Emergency banner patterns, prominent phone numbers
